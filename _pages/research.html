Work in progress

  1. Does unconventional monetary policy impact stock market bubbles?

    2. Abstract: This paper examines the sources of the response of stock prices to unconventional monetary shocks using a Structural Vector autoregressive model. Findings from US data indicate that stock prices and its fundamental component decline in response to a contractionary monetary policy shock. However, there is an increase in its bubble component, which contradicts commonly held beliefs about the impact of monetary policy on bubbles.
